Atmospheric

Using Forecast.io and Flickr Atmospheric is an android weather app that combines one of the best weather data sources with inspiring photographs shot by photographers in your area.

The combination of local photography and informative weather data provides an accurate and changing view of your weather. Atmospheric is powered by forecast.io, giving you the most precise and timely forecasting available.

Atmospheric Premium!

  • Purchase premium to control automatic forecast updating and to configure the frequency of updates.
  • Disable the locked in 3 hour forecast update interval allowing you to select anywhere from 30 mins to 12 hours.
  • Enable automatic forecast updates
  • Enable multiple locations

Standard features include:

  • Notification bar temperature
  • Weather Alerts
  • Pull to refresh
  • Flickr Project Weather images
  • 48 hour and Week long forecasts, with detailed forecast data
  • http://forecast.io weather data
  • Next hour with precipitation by the minute (Availability is location dependent)
  • Multiple language support from Forecast.io (German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Tetum)
  • Muzei Background Source
  • Optional perspective parallax effect
  • Pebble notification support
  • Lock-screen and home-screen widgets
